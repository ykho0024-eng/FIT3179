Personal Safety in Australia

Understanding Violence Patterns Across States and Demographics

Personal safety remains a fundamental aspect
of wellbeing, reflecting how secure people
feel in their daily lives and relationships.
This visual narrative explores trends in
physical and sexual violence in Australia,
using national survey and crime data from
the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS).
Through a series of visualisations, we uncover
how experiences of personal violence have
evolved over time, vary across states, and
differ by gender, relationship, and demographic
factors.
